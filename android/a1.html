Momotaro and the mischievous panda
Momotaro and the Mischievous Panda
A simulation story that pays homage to the story of Momotaro.
At the branching screen, you can proceed to a branching story by selecting the button of the option you want to use.

new game
Start a new game.



continue
It is not displayed on the initial screen.
If you want to continue from the previous page, please select it.
load
You can continue by selecting from the saved data.
save
You can save the current story position.
skip
You can return to any point in the story you have progressed.
Choices
Depending on the button you choose, you will proceed to a branching story.
game

Move the panda and try not to get caught by the enemy.
If you get caught, damage will increase.
The enemies are:
Bears
Monkeys
Foxes
Raccoons
By design, only the panda and monkey can move in the tree area.
In real life, the bear can climb trees, though.
It's written in the Help, so check how it works.
